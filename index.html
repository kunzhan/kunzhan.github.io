Kun Zhan

        

Professor, School of Information Science and Engineering, Lanzhou University

 kzhanlzu.edu.cn, ice.echogmail.com

 Join us!

He received B.S. and Ph.D. degrees from Lanzhou University, in 2005 and 2010, respectively. He was a visiting student with Dalhousie University from 2009 to 2010 and was a visiting scholar with University of Technology Sydney from 2017 to 2018. He is currently with Lanzhou University. Here is his CV.

His general research interests are in machine learning, neural networks, and optimization.
